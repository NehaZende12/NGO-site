Soft skill Training for Youth

For last four years we are organizing Soft Skill Trainings for Youth from Pune urban and rural area. This training has been conducted for 2 days at Pune Urban Gokhalenagar, Kondwa and Rural Kaman Village KhedTaluka. The object of this training is to develop capacity of the youth through leadership, Communication and organizing skill. Till date we have provided these trainings to around 240 youths from both Urban/Rural areas of Pune.
