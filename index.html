About Us

“Dnyandayini Education and Social Organization" is Non-governmental Organization Registered under the Social Development Registration Act in 2007. The Organization is based in Pune and Has been working in all over the Pune district and all over the Maharashtra State.

For last 20 Years Organization is Implementing different programs on Education, Health, economical and social for the overall development of Population especially children, Youth and Women from Pune Urban & Rural Areas of Pune District and Maharashtra region.

Our focus is mainly to improve educational status of the children through our English medium school.

MISSION

Our Mission is to give support to the children and their parents from Rural and Urban areas through establishment of school and Educational trainings, Livelihood trainings providing coordination with the stakeholders. Major concern of the Organization. The main objective of the Organization is to work in the field of Education, Agricultural, and improve the status of underprivileged population from Rural and Urban Areas. Using all the means and tools providing current knowledge and technology for cooperation and aid objective giving maximum priority to Education, Health and Economical development activities.

VISION

Sustained Society where children, adolescent, youth and women improve their Educational status and Motivate to take Initiative of their Holistically Development. Our focus is mainly to improved educational status of the children through our English medium school.
